Our paper "CLEANER: Self-Purified Trajectories Boost Agentic Reinforcement Learning" is accepted to ICLR 2026 SPOT Workshop! [[Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15141)]; [[Code](https://github.com/Tianshi-Xu/Open-CLEANER)]. CLEANER resolves the credit assignment dilemma in agentic RL by training on self-purified trajectories, achieving SOTA performance with just one-third of the training cost.
